If Statements

If statements are extremely simple in Simple Line. if is one question mark and else/else if is two. 

10 > <- 12 > ? > |10 is less| > log
10 > -> 12 > ?? > |10 is more| > log
?? > |10 is equal| > log

You can also use an end marker (?!) to code at the end of an if statement on the same line while still having elses work.

6 > = 10 > ? > |6 = 10| > log > ?! > |this always runs| > log
?? > |this also runs| > log
